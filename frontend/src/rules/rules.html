Hostel Rules & Regulations

1. No student can check in after 10PM.
2. No student can check out before 5AM.
3. No loud noises after 10 PM.
4. Visitors and non students are not allowed in the hostels except on special days.
5. No smoking or alcohol consumption.
6. Respect hostel staff and fellow students.
7. Report any issues to the porters on duty when they occur.
8. Keep valuables safe and secure.
